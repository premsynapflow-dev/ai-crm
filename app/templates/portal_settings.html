How to get your Slack webhook URL:

  1. Go to api.slack.com/apps and click Create New App
  2. Choose From scratch, name it "Neuronyx", pick your workspace
  3. Click Incoming Webhooks → toggle it on
  4. Click Add New Webhook to Workspace → pick a channel → Allow
  5. Copy the webhook URL that starts with https://hooks.slack.com/services/...

Leave blank to remove the integration.
